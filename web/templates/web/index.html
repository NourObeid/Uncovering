UNCOVERING LIVES OF WORLD WAR I























Experiences of the First World War could be profoundly different, depending on the location, time, military unit, and many other variants. This broader project seeks to provide more context for the experiences of different military units in different locations and different stages of the war. In histories, memoirs, diaries, correspondence, which places are mentioned most, and is said about them ?



Why Us

Empowered by is its capacity of detecting place names that aren't mentioned in official gazetteers, It gives the user a more realistic and a more precise image of life at various locations during the war.

Heading

We are collaborating with the British Library and applying Machine learning & Natural Language Processing Techniques to our System, which detects and generate a gazetteer of place names and their description within digitised publications from and about the First World War
