UNCOVERING LIVES OF WORLD WAR I






















Experiences of the First World War could be profoundly different, depending on the location, time, military unit, and many other variants. This broader project seeks to provide more context for the experiences of different military units in different locations of the war. In histories, memoirs, diaries, correspondence,which places are mentioned most and what is said about them ?
